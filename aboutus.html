Our Story

We are a group of scholars from Kode with Klossy who aspire to challenge the bias and disparity in reproductive healthcare. We are all passionate about making a difference, big or small, and envision a more equitable society for everyone. Our names are Kandyce Diep, Anna Kovtunenko, and Kenya Gonzalez.

Mission

The Rose Project aims to bring awareness to racial bias in reproductive and maternal healthcare. The project visualizes data about:

  • The Black maternal health crisis
  • Maternal care deserts
  • Disparities in breast cancer mortality

to paint a clearer picture for our users on the inequalities in our healthcare system. We aspire to educate and encourage people to take action against these injustices.

Racial disparities are present in every system in society. It is likely fueled by structural discrimination against certain demographics that can eventually result in lower socioeconomic status for individuals, making it difficult to recieve the proper treatement & care needed (American Cancer Society). The medical field being prominently affected can lead to mortality differences for women of varying races, making women’s healthcare and treatment a matter of life or death. The Rose Project aims to raise awareness of racial/ethnic biases in healthcare and our goal is to make sure every individual recieves the fundamental treatment they deserve.

Together, we can expose the thorns of the medical injustices and bloom a more equitable future
